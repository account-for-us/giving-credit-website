Social Credit Network

A central component of formal credit markets is that each borrower knows how much credit they have available at any time. Knowing how much credit a potential borrower has available provides a psychological sense of financial security, even when one is not borrowing.

Account For Us reveals social credit lines by enabling people to report how much they would be willing to lend others if they needed a loan. The increased visibility into social credit lines will decrease reliance on payday lenders.
